About us

Who we are

Recognizing the imperative of sustainable growth, After Rain Ventures seeks opportunities ranging from real estate to blockchain and even photography. These shifts underscore our dedication to achieving long-lasting financial returns and contributing positively to technological and societal advancement.

After Rain Ventures intends to direct the assets from its diverse investment vehicles towards real estate internationally, capitalizing on significant tax incentives and established political relationships to optimize our portfolio. Our approach combines asset leverage with strategic investments in QOZs, receiving passive income from rental tenants is a strategy aimed at maximizing financial returns and contributing to urban revitalization. ARV is thus set to enhance its market presence and drive sustainable development in emerging markets, demonstrating our commitment to innovative and impactful investment practices that generate significant returns.
